# Traffic Demand Prediction - Approach Document (Submission T7-T10)

## Problem
Predict traffic demand (continuous 0-1) for urban grid locations at specific time intervals, evaluated using R2 score.

## Deep Insight: Train/Test Distribution Mismatch & GBDT Extrapolation
- **Train Data**: Day 48 (covers all 24 hours) and Day 49 (covers only hours 0 to 2, i.e., 0:00 to 2:00 AM).
- **Test Data**: Day 49 (covers only hours 2:15 to 13:45).
- **Extrapolation Issue**: Since Day 49 train data only has hours 0-2, GBDTs trained on Day 49 cannot extrapolate to daytime peak demand values.
- **Regularization Constraint**: Lower Ridge regularization (e.g., $\alpha=5.0$) overfits polynomial interaction terms on Day 49 train rows, leading to unstable extrapolation at test time. Keeping a higher regularization ($\alpha=50.0$) ensures stable, smooth linear extrapolation.

## Two-Stage Blended Ensemble Solution
To solve the time-of-day distribution shift and GBDT extrapolation limits, we built a hybrid Two-Stage + Global GBDT blended pipeline.

### Model 1: Two-Stage Linear Extrapolation Model (Stable alpha=50.0)
- **Stage 1 (Baseline Profile)**: Train LightGBM and XGBoost models on the full 24 hours of Day 48 using out-of-fold target encodings. We predict Day 49 baseline demand.
- **Lag Feature Mapping**: Map Day 48 demand at the same `(geohash, time_slot)` to Day 49 (`demand_prev_day`).
- **Stage 2 (Calibration/Scale)**: Train a Ridge Regression model (with degree-2 polynomial interactions and $\alpha=50.0$ for stability) on the Day 49 train data (hours 0-2) to scale Stage 1 predictions and lag features to Day 49 levels.

### Model 2: Global GBDT (v3/T2)
- Trains on all data using Bayesian smoothed target encodings in log space.
- Captures non-linear local neighborhood and frequency variations.

### Blends (T7-T10)
- T3 (50/50 blend) scored **87.719** on the leaderboard.
- Increasing the Two-Stage model weight beyond 50% decreased the score (T4 = 87.2, T6 = 86.9, T5 = 86.5).
- This indicates the optimal blend weight peak lies below 50%. We generated blends between 30% and 45% Two-Stage weight:
  - **T7**: 40% Two-Stage / 60% Global GBDT
  - **T8**: 35% Two-Stage / 65% Global GBDT
  - **T9**: 45% Two-Stage / 55% Global GBDT
  - **T10**: 30% Two-Stage / 70% Global GBDT
